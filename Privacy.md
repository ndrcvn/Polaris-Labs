Overview
Glass Calculator ("the App") is a calculator application developed by Andrea Cevenini ("Developer"). Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection
We do not collect any personal data. Glass Calculator:

Does not collect personal information
Does not track your location
Does not access your contacts, photos, or files
Does not use analytics or tracking tools
Does not use cookies
Does not contain advertising or ad tracking
Does not require an account or login

All calculations, history, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to external servers.
Third-Party Services
Glass Calculator does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.
Children's Privacy
Glass Calculator does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
